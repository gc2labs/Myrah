My Favorite Non-Fiction Author: Anne Frank

The Diary of a Young Girl

A picture of Anne Frank

Annelies Marie "Anne" Frank was born in Frankfurt, Germany. She lived most of her life in or near Amsterdam, Netherlands, having moved there with her family at the age of four and a half when the Nazis gained control over Germany.

In July 1942, her family went into hiding in some concealed rooms behind a bookcase in the building where Anne's father, Otto Frank, worked. From then until the family's arrest by the Gestapo in August 1944, she kept a diary she had received as a birthday present, and wrote in it regularly.
